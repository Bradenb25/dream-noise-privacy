Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the ad-free version through the in-app purchase option. Once purchased, all ads will be permanently removed.

Can I play sounds in the background?

Yes! Dream Noise continues playing when you lock your device or switch to other apps.

How do I set a sleep timer?

Use the timer feature within the app to automatically stop playback after a set duration.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. If problems continue, please contact us at the email above.
