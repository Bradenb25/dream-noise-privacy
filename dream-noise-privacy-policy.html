Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Dream Noise ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Free Version with Ads

If you use the free version of our App, we partner with Google AdMob to display advertisements. Depending on your choices, the following data may be collected:

If you accept personalized ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (Ad ID / IDFA)
  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data (ads viewed, clicks)

If you reject personalized ads:

  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • Limited device information
  • Basic app performance data

This data is collected by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC. For more information about how Google uses this data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Paid Version (No Tracking)

If you purchase the ad-free version of our App, no advertising-related data is collected. The AdMob SDK is disabled entirely, and no information is shared with advertising partners.

Your Choices

Tracking Consent

When you first open the App, you will be asked whether you accept or reject ad tracking:

  • Accept: You will receive personalized ads based on your interests and behavior.
  • Reject: You will still see ads, but they will not be personalized. Less data will be collected.

You can change your preference at any time in the App's settings.

Remove All Tracking

You can purchase the ad-free version of the App to completely remove all advertisements and associated tracking. Once purchased, no advertising data will be collected.

Device-Level Controls

You can also limit ad tracking at the device level:

  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads > Delete advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

How Your Data Is Used

Data collected through advertising is used to:

  • Display advertisements (personalized or non-personalized based on your choice)
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud
  • Improve ad relevance (if you opted in)

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob (Free version only)

  • Purpose: Displaying advertisements
  • Data collected: As described above
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

Advertising data is retained according to Google's data retention policies. We do not independently store your personal information on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Security

All data transmitted through our App is encrypted using industry-standard Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocols.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
